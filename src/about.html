About Maison Bergeret

Local, Organic and Testy

What is Maison Bergeret

Local, Organic and Testy are important to us. These ingredients are organic and Whenever possible local. We believe that fresh is best, and financier are highlighting our local agricultural region.

We believe that at the core of our humanity is the fundamental need to gather together and share: an experience, a drink, a meal. For this reason, we have chosen to devote ourselves to the staple items that have a place in every home, on every table. We are producing something that promotes a healthy community and the ecosystem that supports it.
