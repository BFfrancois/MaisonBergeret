About Maison Bergeret

Local, Organic and Tasty

What is Maison Bergeret

Local, Organic and Tasty are important to us. These ingredients are organic and whenever possible local. We believe that fresh is best, and financier are highlighting our local agricultural region.

All items are handcrafted and prepared using traditional French recipes and high organic ingredients. To demonstrate our commitment for quality

We believe that at the core of our humanity is the fundamental need to gather together and share: an experience, a drink, a meal. For this reason, we have chosen to devote ourselves to the staple items that have a place in every home, on every table. We are producing something that promotes a healthy community and the ecosystem that supports it.

We wish to inspire our customers to adopt the French art de vivre and indulge in the pleasures that fine food can bring.
